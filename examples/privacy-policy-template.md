# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** December 20, 2025  
**Last Updated:** December 20, 2025

Welcome to **SHOTHIK AI** ("we," "us," "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our Facebook advertising management platform.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

When you create an account:
- **Email address**
- **Name**
- **Password** (encrypted)
- **Company information** (optional)
- **Billing details** (for paid plans)

### 1.2 Information from Facebook

When you connect your Facebook account, we collect:

#### Through Facebook Login (OAuth):
- **Facebook user ID**
- **Name**
- **Email address**
- **Profile picture**

#### Through Facebook Marketing API:
With your explicit permission, we access:

**ads_management permission:**
- Ad account IDs and names
- Campaign details (names, objectives, budgets, schedules)
- Ad set information (targeting, placements, optimization settings)
- Ad creative content (headlines, descriptions, images, video URLs)
- Ability to create, edit, pause, and delete campaigns

**ads_read permission:**
- Campaign performance metrics (impressions, clicks, conversions)
- Spend data and costs
- Reach and frequency
- Demographics and device data
- Attribution insights

**pages_show_list permission:**
- List of Facebook Pages you manage
- Page names and IDs
- Page roles (admin, editor, etc.)

**pages_read_engagement permission:**
- Post engagement metrics (likes, comments, shares)
- Follower counts
- Page insights data

### 1.3 Automatically Collected Information

- **IP address**
- **Browser type and version**
- **Device information**
- **Operating system**
- **Usage data** (pages visited, features used, time spent)
- **Cookies and similar technologies**

## 5. Your Facebook Data Rights

### 5.1 Permissions We Request

| Permission | Purpose | Can You Deny? |
|------------|---------|---------------|
| `ads_management` | Create and edit campaigns | No (core feature) |
| `ads_read` | View performance metrics | No (core feature) |
| `pages_show_list` | List your Pages | Yes (limits functionality) |
| `pages_read_engagement` | View engagement data | Yes (optional) |

### 5.2 How to Revoke Permissions

**Method 1: From SHOTHIK AI**
- Settings → Connected Accounts → Disconnect Facebook

**Method 2: From Facebook**
- facebook.com → Settings → Apps and Websites → Remove SHOTHIK AI

Revoking permissions will:
- Delete all your Facebook data from our systems
- Disable Facebook-related features
- Keep your SHOTHIK AI account (unless you delete it separately)

## 8. Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)

### 8.1 Rights for EU Users (GDPR)

- **Right to access:** Request a copy of your data
- **Right to rectification:** Correct inaccurate data
- **Right to erasure:** Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Right to restrict processing:** Limit how we use your data
- **Right to data portability:** Export your data
- **Right to object:** Stop processing for certain purposes
- **Right to withdraw consent:** Revoke previously given permissions

### 8.2 Rights for California Users (CCPA)

- **Right to know:** What data we collect and how we use it
- **Right to delete:** Request deletion of your data
- **Right to opt-out:** Stop sale of data (we don't sell data)
- **Right to non-discrimination:** Equal service regardless of privacy choices

### 8.3 How to Exercise Your Rights

Email: **privacy@shothik.ai**

Include:
- Your name and email
- Account details
- Specific request
- Proof of identity (for security)

We respond within **30 days**.
