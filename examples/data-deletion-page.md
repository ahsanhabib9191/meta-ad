# How to Delete Your SHOTHIK AI Data

You have full control over your data. There are three ways to delete your information from SHOTHIK AI:

## Option 1: Disconnect Facebook Account (In-App)

This option removes your Facebook connection while keeping your SHOTHIK AI account.

### Steps:
1. Log in to [SHOTHIK AI](https://shothik.ai)
2. Navigate to **Settings** → **Connected Accounts**
3. Find your Facebook account connection
4. Click **"Disconnect Facebook"**
5. Confirm the action

### What Gets Deleted:
- Your Facebook access token (immediately)
- All synced ad campaigns, ad sets, and ads
- Campaign performance data
- Facebook Page connections
- All Facebook-related analytics

### What Stays:
- Your SHOTHIK AI account and login credentials
- Account settings and preferences
- Billing information (if applicable)

You can reconnect your Facebook account at any time.

---

## Option 2: Delete Your Entire Account

This permanently removes everything, including your SHOTHIK AI account.

### Steps:
1. Log in to [SHOTHIK AI](https://shothik.ai)
2. Go to **Settings** → **Account** → **Delete Account**
3. Read the warning carefully
4. Type the confirmation phrase
5. Click **"Permanently Delete My Account"**

### What Gets Deleted:
- Your SHOTHIK AI account
- All Facebook connections and data
- All campaigns, ad sets, and ads
- Account settings and preferences
- Billing information
- All analytics and reports

**⚠️ This action cannot be undone.**

---

## Option 3: Remove from Facebook.com

You can also delete your data directly from Facebook's settings. This triggers an automatic deletion request to SHOTHIK AI.

### Steps:
1. Go to [facebook.com](https://facebook.com) and log in
2. Click your **profile picture** (top right corner)
3. Select **"Settings & Privacy"** → **"Settings"**
4. In the left sidebar, click **"Apps and Websites"**
5. Find **"SHOTHIK AI"** in the list of active apps
6. Click **"Remove"**
7. Confirm removal when prompted

### What Happens:
- Facebook immediately sends us a deletion request
- We automatically delete your connection within **1 minute**
- All associated data is removed within **24 hours**

## Data Deletion Timeline

Once you initiate deletion (via any method above), here's when your data is removed:

| Data Type | Deletion Timing |
|-----------|----------------|
| **Access tokens** | Immediate (within 1 minute) |
| **Facebook account connection** | Immediate |
| **Campaign metadata** | Within 24 hours |
| **Ad sets and ads data** | Within 24 hours |
| **Performance metrics** | Within 24 hours |
| **Facebook Page data** | Within 24 hours |
| **Anonymized analytics logs** | 30 days (required for compliance) |

### Why 30 days for analytics?
We retain anonymized, aggregated analytics for 30 days to comply with legal and financial reporting requirements. These logs do **not** contain personally identifiable information or Facebook user data.

## What Data We Delete

When you delete your data, we remove:

### From SHOTHIK AI:
- ✅ Your Facebook user ID and access tokens
- ✅ Connected ad account information
- ✅ All campaign data (names, objectives, budgets, schedules)
- ✅ All ad set data (targeting, placements, optimization)
- ✅ All ad creative data (headlines, descriptions, images, videos)
- ✅ Performance metrics (impressions, clicks, conversions, spend)
- ✅ Facebook Page connections
- ✅ Engagement data (likes, comments, shares)
- ✅ AI-generated copy and recommendations

### We DO NOT delete:
- ❌ Your SHOTHIK AI account (unless you chose Option 2)
- ❌ Billing records (required by law for 7 years)
- ❌ Anonymized usage statistics (no personal information)

## Automated Deletion

SHOTHIK AI complies with Facebook's Data Deletion Request API. When you remove our app from Facebook:

1. Facebook sends us a **signed deletion request**
2. Our system **validates** the request signature
3. We **immediately delete** your access token
4. All associated data is **removed within 24 hours**
5. We return a **confirmation code** to Facebook
6. You receive an **email notification**

Our webhook endpoint: `https://shothik.ai/api/facebook/data-deletion`

---

## Data We Cannot Delete

Due to legal requirements, we must retain:

1. **Transaction records** - Financial data for tax purposes (7 years)
2. **Legal compliance logs** - Required by law enforcement or court orders
3. **Anonymized aggregates** - Statistical data with no personal information

These records do **not** include:
- Your name, email, or contact information
- Your Facebook data
- Campaign details or ad content

## Re-Activation

If you reconnect your Facebook account after deletion:

- **Previous data is NOT restored** - We start fresh
- **You'll need to grant permissions again** - Clean slate
- **No historical campaigns** - You'll see only new syncs

Deleted data is permanently removed and cannot be recovered.
